IQAC


INTERNATIONAL QUALITY ASSURANCE CELL (IQAC)

IQAC is a quality sustenance measure.The prime task of the IQAC is to develop a system for conscious, consistent and catalytic improvement in the overall performance of institutions and to promote measures for institutional functioning towards quality enhancement through internalization of quality culture and institutionalization of best practices.
