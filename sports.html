SPORTS

Sports and games are an important aspect of the holistic education that the College offers. Arignar Anna Govt Arts College has excelled in sports and games at all levels, and, encouragement is given to participating students by allowing them flexible continuous assessment schedules and attendance requirements.

Every year a student will be elected as a sports secretary by the college union. Our students participates in various competition which is held in and around our college.

The various sports activities includes:

  • Badminton
  • Throwball
  • Cricket
  • Hockey
  • Khabadi
  • Kho-Kho
  • Football
  • Track Events
