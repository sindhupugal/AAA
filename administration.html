COLLEGE ADMINISTRATION


About the Administration Office

The administration office is functioning with bursar, superintendent,junior assistant, typist and other active members. They undergo training given by the Government and keep themselves updated. All the manual work are automated in our college. The fees collection, scholarship details and all the monetary transactions are done through office. Students get benefited through various scholarships.
