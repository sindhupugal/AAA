OLD STUDENTS ASSOCIATION


OSA

All the students of our college are the members of Old Students Association. The final year students register themselves in this association. Every year OSA day is celebrated by inviting the members of this association. The most prominent member of OSA is Tmt.E.Sundaravalli.,IAS.,Thiruvallur Dist.br
Our students are working in various higher positions as a Govt. Employee, Teacher, Lecturer, Professors, Accountant, Lab technicians, etc.. at various places.
Every year Rs.200 is contributed to the students who are getting first prize in major on College Day.
