Extra-Curricular Activities


NCC

The main aim of this activity is to mould a student into a great leader with the leadership qualities like discipline, honesty, friendship and social responsibility. It is functioning since 1973 by creating a great cadets from our college. It consist of 108 army like students.The cadets go for an yearly training, national day parade and indulge themselves in other activities. The students who had received NCC B and C certificates are given priority for the higher studies and employment.


NSS

It was established by Jawaharlal Nehru to make the students get educated by means of social activities. It aims to develop a socially responsible students. It was started in our college in the year 1976. Now it has 2 units with 200 students in it.

YRC

The Youth Red Cross society helps to conduct various programmes to create an awareness among people.Students involve themselves in Blood donation camp and various social related activies like rally.

Red Ribbon Club

The Red Ribbon Club as a part of NSS conducted various competition among students.

Environmental Club

The Students are sensitised to environmental issues and given every opportunity to make a difference by their commitment to environmental responsibility.

Consumer club

The stationary items, books, notes and other required items are sold to the students by this cooperative store.

Womens Development Cell

The purpose of this facility is to promote the empowerment of women.
