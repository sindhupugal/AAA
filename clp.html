COMPUTER LITERACY PROGRAMME (CLP)

Computer Literacy Programme was started by NITT as a Government programme in the year 2000. By the year 2005 it was handed over to the college for the students to gain computer knowledge. The students other than computer science major can enroll in this programme in the first year. More than 1000 students are getting benefited through this programme. The computer course certificate is provided at the end of the programme which helps the students in job opportunities.
The incharge of CLP is taken care by Mrs. R. Vijaya.,MCA.,M.Phil., Asst.Professor.
